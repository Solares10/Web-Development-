The Birth of Venus is a celebrated painting by Italian Renaissance artist Sandro Botticelli, created around 1485-1486. The artwork depicts the goddess Venus emerging from the sea on a shell, symbolizing her birth. She is surrounded by mythological figures, including the wind gods Zephyrus and Aura, and a handmaiden preparing to clothe her.
